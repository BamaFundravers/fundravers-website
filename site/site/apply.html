Common Questions

Good to know.

Does it cost anything to host a Fundravers event?

No. Fundravers handles all production and coordination costs out of our operational budget. There's no fee to apply or to host an event.

How much can we expect to raise?

It varies by event size, venue, and cause visibility. Our events have raised anywhere from a few hundred to over $20,000. We'll set realistic expectations for your situation.

Do we need to be a registered nonprofit?

No. Student organizations, community groups, athletic programs, and informal causes have all partnered with us.

Where does the 10% overhead go?

Equipment, software, insurance, and small logistics costs. Nothing goes to personal pay. Full breakdowns always posted after each event. See our Impact page.
